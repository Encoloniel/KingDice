
**NAME**
       rolenick - Nicknames the user with the highest role name and their username

**SYNOPSIS**
       rolenick

**DESCRIPTION**
       Takes the highest role the message author has, and gives it as a tag into their username

**EXAMPLE**
```
        '[role.name] message.author.username'
```

**PERMISSIONS**
        User Only
        "CHANGE_NICKNAME"

        Bot Only
        "MANAGE_NICKNAMES"
