
**NAME**
       lowercase - take the whole string and change it to full lowercase

**SYNOPSIS**
       lowercase
       lowercase <word1>
       lowercase <word1> <word2> ...

**DESCRIPTION**
       Uses JavaScript's toLowerCase method to change the words after all to lowercase

**EXAMPLE**
        Hello, my name is King Dice!
        hello, my name is king dice!
```
        <word1> <word2>
        <word1>.toLowerCase() <word2>.toLowerCase()
```
