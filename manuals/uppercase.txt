
**NAME**
       uppercase - take the whole string and change it to full uppercase

**SYNOPSIS**
       uppercase
       uppercase <word1>
       uppercase <word1> <word2> ...

**DESCRIPTION**
       Uses JavaScript's toUpperCase method to change the words after all to uppercase

**EXAMPLE**
        Hello, my name is King Dice!
        HELLO, MY NAME IS KING DICE!
```
        <word1> <word2>
        <word1>.toUpperCase() <word2>.toUpperCase()
```
