
**NAME**
       snakecase - replaces every space by a underscore

**SYNOPSIS**
       snakecase
       snakecase <word1>
       snakecase <word1> <word2>

**DESCRIPTION**
       Replaces a line of string's space code to a underscore

**EXAMPLE**
        Hello, my name is King Dice!
        Hello,_my_name_is_King_Dice!
```
        <word1> <word2>
        <word1>_<word2>
```
