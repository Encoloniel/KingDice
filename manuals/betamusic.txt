
**NAME**
       betabetamusic - plays music from a YouTube link/id

**SYNOPSIS**
       betamusic
       betamusic <join>
       betamusic <leave>
       betamusic <play>
       betamusic <play> <link|id>

**DESCRIPTION**
       Use the command to first make the bot join the voice channel, and lets the bot play the music you have entered the command.

**DISCLAIMER**
        This is a beta feature, so it won't work perfectly. (BetaMusic is a beta feature of the music command.)
