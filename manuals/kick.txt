
**NAME**
       kick - kicks a member from a guild

**SYNOPSIS**
       kick <mention_user>
       kick <mention_user> <kick_reason>

**DESCRIPTION**
       kicks a member from the guild, letting them impossible to join back again unless getting a link to the server. Also DMs the member before the kick with a appeals server link and an information about the kick.

**PERMISSIONS**
        "MEMBER_KICK"

**FLAGS**
        "--silent"
                Do not send any messages to the member that is kicked.
