
**NAME**
       dotcase - replaces every space by a underscore

**SYNOPSIS**
       dotcase
       dotcase <word1>
       dotcase <word1> <word2>

**DESCRIPTION**
       Replaces a line of string's space code to a period (dot)

**EXAMPLE**
        Hello, my name is King Dice!
        Hello,.my.name.is.King.Dice!
```
        <word1> <word2>
        <word1>.<word2>
```
