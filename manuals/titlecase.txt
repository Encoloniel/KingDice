
**NAME**
       titlecase - replaces every first word's character by a uppercase

**SYNOPSIS**
       titlecase
       titlecase <word1>
       titlecase <word1> <word2>

**DESCRIPTION**
       Replaces the first character of every word and changes it to an uppercase letter

**EXAMPLE**
        Hello, my name is King Dice!
        Hello, My Name Is King Dice!
