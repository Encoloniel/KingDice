
**NAME**
       sentencecase - replaces every first sentence's character by a uppercase

**SYNOPSIS**
       sentencecase
       sentencecase <sentence1>
       sentencecase <sentence1> <sentence2>

**DESCRIPTION**
       Replaces the first character of every sentence and changes it to an uppercase letter. Seperated a sentence by a `". "`

**EXAMPLE**
        Hello, my name is King Dice!
        hello, my name is King Dice!
