
**NAME**
       hyphencase - replaces every space by a hyphen

**SYNOPSIS**
       hyphencase
       hyphencase <word1>
       hyphencase <word1> <word2>

**DESCRIPTION**
       Replaces a line of string's space code to a hyphen

**EXAMPLE**
        Hello, my name is King Dice!
        Hello,-my-name-is-King-Dice!
```
        <word1> <word2>
        <word1>-<word2>
```
