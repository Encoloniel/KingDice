
**NAME**
       ban - banishes a user from a guild

**SYNOPSIS**
       ban <mention_user>
       ban <mention_user> <ban_reason>

**DESCRIPTION**
       bans a member from the guild, letting them impossible to join back again unless advised otherwise, or unbanned by an administrator. Also DMs the member before the ban with a appeals server link and an information about the ban.

**PERMISSIONS**
        "MEMBER_BAN"
