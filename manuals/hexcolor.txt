
**NAME**
       hexcolor - shows an image of a searched hex value

**SYNOPSIS**
       hexcolor
       hexcolor <hexValue>

**DESCRIPTION**
       Displays a photo of the hexcolor from http://www.colorhexa.com/ and displays it in a embed with short description about the hex value.

**CONSTRAINTS**
      ```/^[0-9A-F]{6}$/i.test.(<hexValue>)```
